We would like to collect some information about arrival delays of United Airlines (UA) flights. Do the following: pick all UA departures with non-zero arrival delay, calculate the mean arrival delay for each of the corresponding flight numbers, and find the five flights with the largest mean delay.

flights %>% filter(carrier == "UA" & arr_delay != 0) %>% group_by(flight) %>% summarize(mean_delay = mean(arr_delay)) %>% arrange(desc(mean_delay)) %>% slice(1:5)

An alternative acceptable for answer for this question:

flights %>% 
  filter(carrier=="UA" & arr_delay>0) %>%     # pick all UA flights with non-zero delay 
  group_by(flight) %>%                        # group by flight number
  summarize(mean_delay=mean(arr_delay)) %>%   # calculate mean arrival delay
  arrange(desc(mean_delay)) %>%               # arrange in descending order
  slice(1:5)                                  # pick top five
